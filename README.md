# 🧠 AI Nutrition OS

AI-powered Indian nutrition intelligence platform built with FastAPI + Next.js.

AI Nutrition OS delivers personalized meal planning, smart food analysis, adaptive health scoring, medical-aware nutrition filtering, hydration intelligence, and AI-powered coaching in a modern full-stack health platform.

---

# ✨ Features

## 🥗 AI Meal Planning
- Personalized Indian meal plans
- Fat loss / muscle gain / maintenance goals
- Multi-day nutrition generation
- Dynamic macro calculations

## 🧠 AI Nutrition Intelligence
- Dynamic health scoring
- Adaptive metabolic strategy
- Goal-aware coaching system
- Smart meal quality analysis

## 🛡️ Medical Safety Shield
- Diabetes-aware filtering
- Kidney-safe protein adjustments
- Elderly-safe meal planning
- Pregnancy-safe nutrition overrides
- Low BP / thyroid safety logic
- Unsafe food replacement engine

## 💧 Lifestyle & Wellness Analytics
- Hydration tracking
- Sleep analysis
- BMI / BMR / TDEE analytics
- Macro balance scoring

## 📸 Smart Food Scanner
- AI-powered food image scanning
- Groq Vision integration
- Gemini Vision fallback
- Instant calorie + macro estimation
